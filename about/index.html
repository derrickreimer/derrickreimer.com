Hey there!

My name is Derrick Reimer and I’m a full-stack developer living in Minneapolis. I’ve been building software products for the last decade and writing code since the days of MS-DOS. Most recently, I founded Codetree and co-founded Drip (acquired by Leadpages in 2016).

I am currently building Level, an alternative to real-time chat for software teams. I co-host The Art of Product Podcast with Ben Orenstein, where we discuss all things related to building and growing software products (and other completely irrelevant topics).

This site is a compilation of things I have learned along the way, through the lens of a technical founder.
